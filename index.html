About Us

BobiCraft Indonesia is a leading crochet store that offers high-quality handmade crochet products. We are passionate about preserving the art of crochet and bringing the beauty of crochet to our customers.

Our team of skilled artisans carefully crafts each piece with love and attention to detail. We take pride in promoting the traditional art of crochet and supporting local craftsmanship.
